About A+R

Appreciate + Reciprocate is a community of Michigan scholarship students dedicated to providing a space for recipents of scholarships to address the specific challenges they face as well as enriching the cycle of giving from which they have benefited, through community service, fundraising, and raising awareness on access to education on our campus.


Over the past view years A+R members have gotten together and volunteered at Leuk's Landing petting cats with feline leukemia, preparing dinner with Ronald McDonald House Charities for families of children receiving treatment at a local area health facility, ushering at the Michigan Theater, and so much more.


"Making College Happen" is A+R's very own volunteer program. Thinking back to our own experiences coming to the University of Michigan and recieving scholarships, we wanted to share our knowledge with high school students. On March 15th, 2019, A+R launched our pilot volunteer program! We went to Jackson High School and presented to students about various financial resources. We are aiming to go to more schools in the future to continue to make a difference and spread awareness about access to education!

Donate

Appreciate + Reciprocate is very dedicated to enriching the cycle of giving. One way in which we accomplish this is by raising money for our endowed scholarship fund. Gifts to this fund will support scholarships for LSA Students with financial need, academic merit, and a long standing demonstrated passion for serving others through community service.
